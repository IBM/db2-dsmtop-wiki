<h2>Purpose</h2>
<p>The overview tells you at a glance how busy the monitored database is.  It provides basic information about the current level of activity.  For example:</p>
<ul ><li>Number of connections</li><li>Memory consumption</li><li>I/O efficiency</li><li>Indicators of contention such as high lock wait time, lock escalations or deadlocks.</li></ul>
<p>This is the view you see when you start dsmtop.</p>

<p><img alt="DB-Overview.png" lconnwikimacro="image" src="../images/DB-Overview.png" title="DB-Overview.png"/></p>

</li><li class="li" style="margin-top: 0em; margin-bottom: 0em; margin-left: 0em !important;">
<p><span style="font-size:12px">An application may be locking tables explicitly when system-generated row locks may be sufficient</span></p>
</li><li class="li" style="margin-top: 0em; margin-bottom: 0em; margin-left: 0em !important;">
<p><span style="font-size:12px">An application may be using an inappropriate isolation level when binding</span></p>
</li><li class="li" style="margin-top: 0em; margin-bottom: 0em; margin-left: 0em !important;">
<p><span style="font-size:12px">Catalog tables are locked for repeatable read</span></p>
</li><li class="li" style="margin-top: 0em; margin-bottom: 0em; margin-left: 0em !important;">
<p><span style="font-size:12px">Applications are getting the same locks in different orders, resulting in deadlock.</span></p>
</li></ul>
