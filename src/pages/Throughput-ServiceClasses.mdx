<h2>Purpose</h2>
<p>Show how much work is happening in each WLM service class.</p>
<p>One of the main purposes of WLM service classes is to apply different priorities and or policies to different types of work.  For example, limiting the number of large queries allowed to run concurrently.  Service classes are also used to give higher priority to business critical applications.</p>
<p>Validating that service classes are serving their intended purpose requires monitoring the performance of activities that are running in them.</p>
<p>Each row in this view shows a service subclass.  You can use filtering or sorting to group the service subclasses by service superclass.</p>
