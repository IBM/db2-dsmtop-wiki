<h2>Purpose</h2>
<p>Connections (sometimes also known as sessions) are tied to users and applications.  This view shows who is connected, what applications they are running and how much of various resources they are consuming.</p>

<p><span style="font-family:'helvetica neue' , 'helvetica' , 'arial' , sans-serif">Source: </span><span style="font-family:'courier new' , 'courier' , monospace">mon_get_activity.query_cost_estimate</span></p>

<u>Internal Application</u></p>
<p>"Yes" if DB2 considers this an internal connection, "No" for user initiated connections.</p>
<p>This is available only in DB2 v10.5 and higher.  For older versions of DB2, dsmtop shows "?".</p>
