title: Locking - Blockers and Waiters
---

<h2>Purpose</h2>
<p>This card shows transactions that are blocking others.  In scenarios where work is hung, waiting on locks, this shows you the root cause of the problem.  You can easily see which connections could be forced to resolve the hang.</p>

<u>Lock Wait Time of Blocked Transactions (ms)</u></p>
<p>The total time spent waiting on locks for all transactions currently waiting on this lock.</p>
<p>This time includes time previously spent waiting on other locks.</p>
